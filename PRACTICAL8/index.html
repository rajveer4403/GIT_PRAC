Vision

Marwadi University envisions creating globally competent professionals with innovative minds, ethical values, and leadership skills to positively contribute to society and industry. Our vision includes advancing research, nurturing entrepreneurship, and promoting sustainable development practices.

Mission

1. Provide high-quality education with research-focused curriculum.
2. Foster a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship.
3. Promote ethical values, leadership, and holistic development.
4. Collaborate with industry to enhance employability.
5. Encourage community engagement and social responsibility.

Event Gallery

Our university hosts annual cultural festivals, technical fests, workshops, seminars, and guest lectures. Students actively participate in competitions, hackathons, and social initiatives, capturing experiences in a detailed event gallery showcasing achievements and vibrant campus life.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSO)

- Graduates will acquire domain-specific knowledge and practical skills.
- Ability to analyze, design, and implement solutions efficiently.
- Work collaboratively in teams and independently on projects.
- Develop professional ethics, leadership, and social responsibility.
- Adapt to emerging technologies and industry trends.

Faculty of Engineering & Technology (FOET)

The FOET at Marwadi University offers cutting-edge engineering programs with modern labs, research centers, and experienced faculty members. Departments include:
- Computer Engineering
- Mechanical Engineering
- Civil Engineering
- Electrical Engineering
- Electronics & Communication
- Information Technology

Faculty members are engaged in research, consultancy, and industry collaboration, ensuring students gain practical knowledge, innovative thinking, and hands-on experience.
