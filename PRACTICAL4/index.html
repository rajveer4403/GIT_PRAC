VISION

1) To emerge as a globally recognized center of excellence in higher education, research, and innovation, nurturing future leaders who contribute to society with knowledge, ethics, and sustainability.
2) We aspire to be an institution where creativity meets technology, where students are empowered with skills, values, and the spirit of entrepreneurship to address global challenges and improve the quality of life.

MISSION

To provide high-quality education in engineering, science, and technology through innovative teaching and learning methodologies.

To foster a research-oriented environment that promotes creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills among students and faculty.

To collaborate with industries and research organizations for developing technologies that serve society.

To instill ethical values, leadership qualities, and social responsibility in students, making them competent professionals and responsible citizens.

To encourage innovation, entrepreneurship, and lifelong learning for personal and professional growth.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSO)

PSO 1: Graduates will be able to apply fundamental knowledge of mathematics, science, and engineering to design, develop, and analyze real-world engineering problems.

PSO 2: Graduates will have the ability to use modern engineering tools, techniques, and software to develop solutions for complex systems with societal and environmental considerations.

PSO 3: Graduates will demonstrate leadership, communication, teamwork, and entrepreneurial skills to succeed in multidisciplinary projects and professional careers.

PSO 4: Graduates will commit to lifelong learning, ethical practices, and sustainability while contributing positively to society and the global environment.
